Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and saut√© the onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add the curry powder and stir well.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 25 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice.
