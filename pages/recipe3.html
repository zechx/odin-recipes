Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking tin.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving.
